QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Have more questions? Don't hesitate to reach out to our team.

Can I try StoryForge Pro before subscribing?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial on our Standard and Professional plans with no credit card required. You can also start with our Free plan to get a feel for the basic features before upgrading.

Can I switch between plans?

Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. If you upgrade, the new features will be available immediately. If you downgrade, your current features will remain until the end of your billing cycle.

Is there a discount for students or educational institutions?

Yes, we offer special pricing for students, teachers, and educational institutions. Please contact our support team with valid ID or institutional credentials to apply for academic pricing.

What happens to my work if I cancel my subscription?

Your work remains yours. If you cancel a paid subscription, you'll be downgraded to the Free plan and will still have access to your projects, but with limited features. We recommend exporting your work before downgrading if you use advanced features extensively.

Is there a limit to how many words I can write?

No, there are no word count limits on any of our plans. The limits mentioned in the pricing tiers refer to the number of active projects you can work on simultaneously, not the length of your writing.
